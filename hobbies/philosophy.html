Razors

Adler's Razor (also known as Newton's flaming laser sword)
If something cannot be settled by experiment or observation, then it is not worthy of debate.
Hanlon's Razor
Never attribute to malice that which can be adequately explained by stupidity.
Hitchen's Razor
That which can be asserted without evidence can be dismissed without evidence.
Occam's Razor
Explanations that require fewer unjustified assumptions are more likely to be correct; avoid unnecessary or improbable assumptions.
